About Lalo

From vocalist to producer, rooted in the studio

I started out as a drummer, guitarist and bassist, then as a vocalist, and that path led me to production — that's where I found my true love. Since then I've been involved in countless projects that have shaped my professional experience.

Over my 11 years of experience I've worked as a live sound engineer, and produced, recorded, mixed and mastered music across all kinds of genres — from acoustic and pop/rock, through urban genres like reggaeton, trap and rap, to metal and hardcore. Whatever your style, I know how to understand your vision and help you achieve an improved version of what you're looking for.

I work in Barcelona, right in the city centre, just a 10-minute drive from the Sagrada Familia, where I hold in-person vocal recording, mixing and mastering sessions alongside my online services.
